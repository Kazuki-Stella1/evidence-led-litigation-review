# Fictional sample declaration

Every person, organization, date, transaction, medical event, amount, and exhibit in `sample/` is deliberately fictional and exists only to demonstrate deterministic checks. `workflow/` demonstrates mixed-evidence intake, mapping, logic, and an integrated proofreading pass. `proofreading/` demonstrates seven exact-location typo, conversion, bracket, and exhibit candidates in Markdown, JSON, and CSV.

- `原告A` and `取引相手B` are labels, not pseudonyms for real parties.
- The commercial repayment dispute was invented for this repository.
- The sports injury and sleep-disorder example was invented to demonstrate separation of medical events.
- The evidence files are fictional JSON and plain-text fixtures only; no real exhibit image or document is included.

Do not replace these files with real case material in a public clone or public fork.
